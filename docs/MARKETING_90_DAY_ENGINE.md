# Locklet (P.S.) — 90-Day Multi-Channel Marketing Engine

**Role framing:** Lead Growth Marketer + Brand Strategist, high-end Privacy Tech.

**Product core:** Vault-based messaging and file storage; heartbeat/check-in trigger; zero-knowledge (AES-256) encryption.

**Aesthetic:** Dark Luxury, “Quiet Security,” Noir.

**Audience:** Actionable for a solo founder.

### YouTube Shorts — three scripts (~15s, “silent failsafe”)

**Script A — The missed ping**

- **0–3s:** Black screen. Text: “Day 14. No check-in.”
- **3–8s:** Phone lock screen → subtle app icon. VO (whisper): “You already decided what happens next.”
- **8–12s:** Vault glyph animates; text: “Encrypted. Recipient-defined. Zero-knowledge.”
- **12–15s:** End card: “Locklet — your silent failsafe.” + App Store lockup.

**Script B — The key you never shared**

- **0–4s:** Close-up: hand closes a safe. Text: “You didn’t share your keys.”
- **4–9s:** Heartbeat UI: simple toggle/check-in. VO: “You don’t share them daily. You release them—if you have to.”
- **9–15s:** Text stack: AES-256 • Vault • Trigger → “P.S.”

**Script C — The conversation you’re not here for**

- **0–5s:** Empty chair, window rain. Text: “The hardest message is the one you’re not there to send.”
- **5–11s:** App: compose → encrypt → schedule/trigger. VO: “Pre-authorized. Pre-encrypted. Pre-planned.”
- **11–15s:** CTA: “Download Locklet.”

## Task 3: Influencer “trust” campaign

### Tiered outreach

| Tier                      | Who                                                                                                                                  | Goal                 | Offer                                                                                  | Deliverable                                                               |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **T1 — High-trust micro** | Opsec educators, privacy lawyers (personal brands), investigative freelancers, solo travel safety creators (10k–80k, high save rate) | Credibility transfer | No cash first: 90-day Pro + private office-hours walkthrough; optional affiliate later | 1× technical deep-dive (60–90s) + pinned comment with Canary + whitepaper |
| **T2 — Niche mid**        | Crypto estate planners, family estate attorneys (content arms), digital legacy coaches                                               | SEO + long trust     | Co-branded checklist PDF (“Inheritance of access”) + backlink                          | Newsletter or long video chapter + link to `/crypto` or `/family`         |
| **T3 — High-reach**       | Big lifestyle / travel / news commentary                                                                                             | Volume               | Paid usage rights bundle (whitelisted clips only)                                      | 1× integrated mention + disclosed #ad                                     |

### Vetting: high-trust vs high-reach

| Dimension         | High-trust (prioritize)                                                  | High-reach (use carefully)                                 |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Audience behavior | High saves, long watch, comments asking “how,” DMs from pros             | High views, low saves, viral noise                         |
| Content history   | Explained threat models before; corrected past mistakes                  | Generic “life hacks,” undisclosed promos                   |
| Disclosure        | Clean #ad ethics; separates review from sponsorship                      | Shady past or vague finance promos                         |
| Fit               | Has stake (journalists, travelers, custody topics)                       | Broad entertainment only                                   |
| Verification      | Email domain match, cross-check two platforms, Wayback or pinned sources | Demand raw retention screenshot + comment sentiment sample |
| Risk              | Lower reputational tail-risk if product disappoints                      | Higher—contract fact-check clause on claims you approve    |

**Practical vetting stack (~1 hour per candidate):** (1) last 10 posts for accuracy, (2) comment quality, (3) prior brand disclosures, (4) search “[name] scam / controversy,” (5) personalized angle in pitch (quote one video).

## Task 4: AI-driven scaling (luxury look, under $10k production)

1. **Midjourney / SDXL — “Quiet Security” stills + motion**
   - **Use:** Hero backgrounds, noir textures, abstract vault geometry, rain glass, ledger macro (non-branded).
   - **Workflow:** Lock a style sheet (palette, grain, lens)—generate master stills, then Ken Burns in CapCut/DaVinci; overlay real UI screen recordings (trust) on AI plates (mood).
   - **Guardrail:** AI for environment only; never fake UI or fake endorsements.

2. **HeyGen (or similar) — localized trust explainers**
   - **Use:** 60s “how heartbeat works” in EN + DE + ES with same B-roll from (1).
   - **Workflow:** Script from whitepaper section headers; avatar low dominance (calm, suited minimal); burn in key terms: client-side, you hold keys, we can’t read.
   - **Guardrail:** Label as explainer; send skeptics to canonical doc pages.

3. **Veo / video diffusion — short tension B-roll**
   - **Use:** 3–5s loops: rain on window, hallway light flicker, safe dial abstract, airport dusk—seamless under VO.
   - **Workflow:** Generate 4:5 and 9:16 variants; grade to one LUT; pair with real app capture and legal on-screen disclaimers where needed.
   - **Guardrail:** Avoid realistic faces of strangers or impersonation; stay symbolic.

**Solo-founder bundle:** One locked visual system + 10 AI plates + 5 screen recordings + HeyGen voiceover variants = weekly content without a set.

## 90-day engine — how the pieces connect

- **Days 1–30:** Ship four slugs, canary cadence, one whitepaper PDF; run 2 RSAs; post 3 Shorts/week + daily 8s loops from AI B-roll + real UI.
- **Days 31–60:** T1 outreach (20–40 contacts), lead magnet checklist, retarget whitepaper readers with App Store.
- **Days 61–90:** T2 partnerships, one case-study style thread (anonymous), narrow keyword winners 2× budget.
